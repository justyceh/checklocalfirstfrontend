# CheckLocalFirst — Premium Features

## Overview

CheckLocalFirst operates on a two-sided marketplace model: local businesses and local users. Premium tiers exist for both sides, and their value is interconnected — the more premium users there are, the more valuable business premium becomes, and vice versa. The core identity of the platform is **shop local, support your community**, and every premium feature should reinforce that mission rather than contradict it.

---

## Business Premium

Targeted at local business owners who want to grow their presence, reach loyal local customers, and prove that being on CheckLocalFirst is working for them.

### Promotions & Deals
- Create time-limited promotions or discounts that appear on their business listing
- Deals are surfaced to premium users on the platform (browse deals page, featured on home page)
- Business controls the deal details: discount amount, description, expiration date, redemption limit
- Claimed deals are tracked so the business can see real engagement

### Analytics Dashboard
- View count on their listing page over time
- Number of users who have favorited their business
- Number of deal claims and redemptions
- Insight into how locals are finding and engaging with their listing
- Data is captured from day one so there is meaningful history when they upgrade

### Customer Reach via Newsletter
- When a user favorites a business or claims one of their deals, they opt into updates from that business
- Business premium owners can send a newsletter or update through the CheckLocalFirst platform
- Emails are sent by CheckLocalFirst on behalf of the business — user emails are never exposed directly
- Keeps businesses connected to their most engaged local customers without requiring them to manage their own email system

### Shop Local Badge
- A verified "Local Business" badge displayed prominently on their listing
- Signals to the community that this is a real, active, Reno-based business investing in the platform
- Differentiates premium listings visually from unclaimed or inactive ones

---

## User Premium

Targeted at locals who are committed to shopping local and want to get more out of the platform in return.

### Access to Deals & Promotions
- Claim exclusive deals and discounts posted by premium businesses
- Non-premium users can see that deals exist but cannot claim them — creates a natural upgrade prompt
- Deals are browsable by category, newest, or by favorited businesses

### Business Newsletter Opt-In
- When a user favorites a business or claims a deal, they can opt into updates from that business
- Receive newsletters and promotions sent directly through CheckLocalFirst
- Full control to unsubscribe from individual businesses at any time

### Favorites
- Save businesses to a personal favorites list (available to all users, free tier)
- Premium users get enhanced favorites: deal alerts when a favorited business posts a promotion, and priority in newsletter targeting for businesses they follow

---

## The Closed Loop

The premium model is designed so both sides reinforce each other:

1. A local user signs up and favorites businesses they care about
2. A business sees favorites growing in their analytics dashboard
3. Business upgrades to premium to reach those users with a deal
4. User sees the deal, claims it, visits the business
5. Money stays local, community strengthens, platform proves its value

This loop is what separates CheckLocalFirst from Google Business Profile or Yelp — it is not just a directory, it is an active connection between locals who want to support their community and businesses who are part of it.

## Pricing (Planned)

Pricing has not been finalized. Target range:

- **Business Premium:** $15–25/month — low enough to be an easy yes for a local business owner
- **User Premium:** $3–5/month — low friction, community-contribution framing ("support local for less than a coffee")

Early adopter pricing and free trial periods for businesses are recommended during the launch phase to build supply-side momentum before charging.

## Rollout Order

Features will be built and launched in this order:

1. Favorites (free, both tiers — foundation for everything else)
2. Promotions & Deals (business creates, premium user claims)
3. Analytics Dashboard (data captured now, surfaced at launch)
4. Newsletter / Customer Reach system
5. Stripe integration and paywall enforcement
6. Shop Local Badge

---

## What Premium Is Not

- Premium is not a replacement for the free tier. Free businesses get a real listing. Free users get real discovery. Premium amplifies, it does not gatekeep the core value.
- Premium does not sell or expose user data. Businesses reach customers through the platform, not around it.
- Premium is not Yelp. There are no ratings, no public reviews, no mechanisms that let the crowd judge or punish a local business. The platform exists to connect and support, not rank and filter.
